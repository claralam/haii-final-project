Real or Fake Job?

What is this app for?

This app helps people identify whether a job is a real job posting or a fraudulent one. With more people searching for jobs virtually, there are also more cyber criminals using fraudulent job postings to steal money and personal information from people. Being able to detect a false job posting could help prevent people from giving out information to false job postings.

How does this app work?

  1. Click on the "Model" tab
  2. Input information about the job posting
  3. Click on the "Predict" button and await results!

Privacy and Data Usage

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information or data from its users. The app is designed to operate solely on the device it is installed on, and all data used by the app is processed on the device itself.

The app does not collect or store any information that can be used to identify its users, such as users' names, email addresses, or IP addresses. No information about users' devices, such as its hardware or software specifications, are stored either.
